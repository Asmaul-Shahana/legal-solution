The Legal Practice Area

There are several types of law you'll come across during your legal studies. On this page, you'll find a comprehensive list of different areas of law you can choose to specialise in.

Business Law

Business law is a crucial component of law in general since, without it, the corporate, manufacturing, and retail sectors would all be under a dictators rule.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is only one of the devices by which organized societies protect the security of individual interests and ensure the survival of the group.

Child Support

Our team is here to provide an accurate assessment of your childs needs and rights prior to getting child support. Our team is prepared to help you with your family law matters.

Education Law

Education law is a set of rules and regulations that guide the workings of an education system. Many regions have detailed educational law systems.

Divorce Law

Divorce is the process of terminating a marriage or marital union. It is also known as dissolution of marriage. Divorce allows each former partner to marry another person.

Tax Law

Tax law, body of rules under which a public authority has a claim on taxpayers, requiring them to transfer to the authority part of their income or property.
